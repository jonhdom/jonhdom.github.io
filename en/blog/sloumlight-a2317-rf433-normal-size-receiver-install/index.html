SLOUMLIGHT A2317 RF433 Normal Size Receiver Install

  • By Admin
  • at March 25, 2024

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. Work volts: AC 85V~240V

  2. Max current: 10A

  3. Standby power: less than 0.15W

  4. RF working frequency: 433.92MHz

  5. Remote control distance: In open area: more than 50 meters. Indoor: 30 meters

  6. Storage switch quantity: no more than 10pcs wireless switches

  7. Size: L47 x W42 x H23 mm

WIRING DIAGRAM

## NOTES

  1. Be safe when wiring and always keep the power off when wiring.

  2. L and N line cannot be reversed.

  3. When using wireless electronics, take care to avoid metal objects, large electronic devices, electromagnetic fields and other sources of strong interference.

  4. Replace the battery in time when the wireless switch voltage is low to get stronger signal transmission.

HOW TO PAIR

  1. Press the learning button (on the receiver) 2 times.

  2. The indicator LED on the receiver will light up.

  3. Then press one button (on the wireless switch).

  4. The indicator on the receiver will flash several times and then turn off. Succeed.

NOTE: If you have more than one wireless switch, you must configure each wireless switch once by following the above steps.

RESET

  1. Press the learning button 8 times (on the receiver).

  2. Succeed.

Notice: After reset, all the wireless switches cannot control this receiver anymore.

TROUBLESHOOTING

If the devices are not working normally, please follow these steps:

  1. Check Remote Battery: The most common issue. If the remote’s indicator LED is dim or doesn’t light up, replace the battery.

  2. Verify Wiring: Double-check all wiring connections to ensure they are correct and secure.

  3. Reprogram Devices: Perform a reset and then re-pair the remote and receiver. This often resolves issues.

  4. Check Environment: Avoid mounting near large metal objects or strong sources of electromagnetic interference.

  5. If the problem still remains, please contact us in time. We will do our best to solve your problem.
