SOLUMLIGHT A2321 DC Relay Remote Control Switch Manual

  • By Admin
  • at February 21, 2022

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. Input / Output Voltage: DC 6V-30V / 1-250V

  2. Quiescent Current: < 5mA

  3. Max Current: 10A

  4. RF Frequency: 433MHz

  5. Working Temperature: -30°C - +80°C

  6. Receiving Sensitivity: >97dBm

  7. Max Paired Remotes: 20 pieces

  1. Supported Encoding: 1527 Learning code, 2262 Fixed code

  2. RF Operating Mode: ASK superheterodyne wireless reception

  3. Remote Control Range: > 50 meters (in open space)

  4. Operating Modes: Momentary / Toggle / Latched

  5. Output Type: Passive (Potential-Free)

WIRING DIAGRAM

Terminal Descriptions:

  • VCC, GND: DC Power Input (6V-30V)

  • NO1, NO2: Normally Open terminals

  • COM1, COM2: Common terminals

  • NC1, NC2: Normally Closed terminals

OPERATING MODES & PROGRAMMING

GENERAL NOTES

  1. The receiver and transmitter are usually pre-paired at the factory. If not, please reprogram them.

  2. Reset the receiver before changing the operating mode. This will clear all previously paired remotes.

  3. Slightly stretch the antenna on the receiver for optimal signal reception.

  4. Replace the remote’s battery promptly if the indicator is dim for stronger signal transmission.

  5. The output is passive (potential-free), meaning it is isolated from the control circuit.

  6. For Latched Mode, a remote with at least 2 buttons is required.

1. MOMENTARY MODE

Operation: Relay turns ON while the button is pressed and held; turns OFF immediately when the button is released.

How to Program:

  1. Press the learning button on the receiver 1 time. The indicator LED will turn OFF.

  2. Press the desired button (e.g., “A”) on the remote transmitter.

  3. The indicator LED will flash several times and then turn OFF.

  4. Press another button (e.g., “B”) on the remote. The LED will flash and turn ON. Success.

2. TOGGLE MODE

Operation: Press button “A” to turn the relay ON; press the same button “A” again to turn it OFF.

How to Program:

  1. Press the learning button on the receiver 2 times. The indicator LED will turn OFF.

  2. Press the desired button (e.g., “A”) on the remote transmitter.

  3. The indicator LED will flash several times and then turn OFF.

  4. Press another button (e.g., “B”) on the remote. The LED will flash and turn ON. Success.

3. LATCHED MODE

Operation: Press button “A” to turn Relay 1 ON; press button “B” to turn Relay 2 ON and Relay 1 OFF.

How to Program:

  1. Press the learning button on the receiver 3 times. The indicator LED will turn OFF.

  2. Press button “A” on the remote transmitter. The LED will flash and turn OFF.

  3. Press button “B” on the remote transmitter. The LED will flash several times and then turn ON. Success.

RESET

  1. Press the learning button on the receiver 8 times.

  2. The indicator LED will flash several times and then turn ON. Success.

IMPORTANT: After reset, all paired remote controls will be erased and must be reprogrammed.

TROUBLESHOOTING

If the devices are not working normally, please follow these steps:

  1. Check Remote Battery: The most common issue. If the remote’s indicator LED is dim or doesn’t light up, replace the battery.

  2. Verify Wiring: Double-check all wiring connections to ensure they are correct and secure.

  3. Reprogram Devices: Perform a reset and then re-pair the remote and receiver. This often resolves issues.

  4. Check Environment: Avoid mounting near large metal objects or strong sources of electromagnetic interference.

  5. If the problem still remains, please contact us in time. We will do our best to solve your problem.
